Cynoptyx

Cynoptyx is an intelligence enabled software platform that generates video synopsis of hour-long videos.

Intelligent video summaries

Cynoptyx makes use of deep learning algorithms to generate a visually coherent video summary of hour-long videos. Now you can watch your favourite sports match just in and under a minute or review your boring surveillance videos in just seconds! 

Reliable and Scalable

Now you can generate highlights of your favourite sports matches, weed out unnecessary video feeds from your surveillance system, make interactive movie trailers and much more!

Artifice

Artifice is an intelligence enabled surveillance system that helps detect and localise Anomalies, Unusual activities, Irregularities in surveillance videos.

Real-Time

Our solution works in Real-Time and works on either pre-recorded surveillance videos or you can just ask your surveillance cameras to feed the data directly into our system, and watch it root out all anomalies.

Easily deployable

You can either choose to add our system as a plugin for your already existing surveillance systems or you can go for the complete solution - that includes just a camera and a Raspberry Pi board.
